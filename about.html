About Meetxport

We exist so exporters spend time closing deals, not chasing them.

Meetxport connects Indian exporters with genuine international buyers — through exclusive, done-for-you outreach and verified, product-matched leads.

The problem we solve

Sourcing buyers shouldn't cost more time than making the product.

Finding genuine international buyers means hundreds of emails, LinkedIn messages and follow-ups, repeated week after week. Most exporters are running production, quality and logistics at the same time — there simply isn't room left for consistent outbound.

Meetxport exists to close that gap: either by running the outreach for you, exclusively and transparently from your own accounts, or by handing you a verified list of buyers who actually match what you sell.

How we operate

Three things we don't compromise on

01

Exclusivity

A buyer approached for you is never pitched the same week to another client. No shared lists, no double-selling.

02

Transparency

Outreach runs from your own approved Gmail and LinkedIn account, so every message and reply belongs to you, visible in real time.

03

Accountability

Weekly progress reports and a standing review call — the pipeline is something you can see, not something you have to trust blindly.

Working with Meetxport

What the engagement actually looks like

Week 1 — Research & setup

We study your product, MOQ and target markets, and connect to your approved Gmail and LinkedIn.

Week 2 onward — Outreach & follow-up

Personalized emails and LinkedIn messages go out, with structured follow-ups on Day 4, 5, 15 and beyond.

Every week — Reporting & review

You receive a progress report and sit in on a review call to steer strategy for the week ahead.

When a buyer replies — Meeting scheduled

We coordinate timing and logistics. You attend the meeting and present your products — we handle everything before that.
